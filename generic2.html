Data Visualization & Regression Analysis in R

R (RStudio)

Goal:
The purpose of this project is to practice analyzing development implications of a social enterprise as part of an Economic Development course. I designed a study to evaluate the effects of probiotic yogurt on malnutrition in children in Uganda. As part of the course, I interpret the data and results of the experiment as if it were real.

Data:
I used a randomized controlled trial design to evaluate the impact of probiotic consumption on children’s health and development. Using R in RStudio, I simulated data to estimate the treatment effect of the RCT. This data is used for the difference-in-difference regression analysis towards the end of the project.

I sourced the remainder of the data from UNICEF and the World Health Organization. It consists of the primary outcome variables, height, weight, and diarrhea frequency, as well as several control variables, including: gender, parents’ heights, upper-arm circumference (a measure of malnutrition), and existing medical conditions (among others).

Outcomes:
Note: These outcomes are based on simulated data, not facutal.
  • The consumption of probiotic yogurt has the potential to reduce the occurrence of child-stunting in Uganda.
  • There is (on average) a 2 inch increase in height for children aged 1-5 years who were in the treatment group.
  • Weight increased by 2.5 pounds for children in the treatment group.
  • Diarrhea frequency for children in the treatment group was 3 episodes, while for those in the control group it was 4 episodes per year.
