About Me

Hi, my name is Sasha! I recently graduated from Santa Clara University, where I double majored in Mathematics and Economics.

I decided to pursue a career in data analytics after taking several econometrics courses during university. Through my interest in economic development, I realized the powerful role of data analytics in solving various issues in underdeveloped countries, and I learned that it is a valuable tool for helping others. Data analytics encourages me to think outside the box, and I love the challenge of coming up with creative solutions to these types of problems.

Aside from my interest in data analytics, I love to travel. To date, I have traveled
to 12 different countries and I hope to visit many more in the future! I also enjoy
volunteering and have spent many years tutoring at non-profit organizations in
the Bay Area.

If you want to get in touch, feel free to reach out at sashaaibara@gmail.com!
