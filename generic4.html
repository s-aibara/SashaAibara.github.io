Data Exploration in SQL: Video Games

SQL(PostgreSQL), Tableau

Goal:
In this simple exploratory data analysis project, I examine data on video game sales and video game reviews in order to determine when video games were the most popular, and which games and platforms were the most successful between the years 1977-2020.

Data:
The data for this project was obtained from Kaggle. It includes the following variables: game, critic score, user score, platform, developer, publisher, release year, and the number of games sold (per game).

Outcomes:
  • The most popular years for video games (according to both critics and users) were 1998, 2002, and 2008.
  • The most successful platform during these years (according to critics) was the Wii, with 77.63 million games sold for the platform, but users preferred the PSP.
  • The most loved game by users during these years was Super Smash Bros. Brawl for Wii, and it sold 13.32 million copies.
